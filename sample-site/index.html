Framework sample pages

These demonstrate a range of ways you can make use of the EBI styles and framework.

Boilerplates

You can use these as templates for your code.

  1. Blank
    big, empty space between header and footer
  2. Ultra wide
    for content with large table or graphics that benefit from using as much screen space as they can get
  3. TO COME - Right sidebar
    for content-driven pages deeper in your site's hierarchy, the sidebar could contain secondary navigation
  4. TO COME - Four columns
    an example of dividing the page into four broad content areas
  5. TO COME - Search results
    an example page with filters or facets for your results in a left-hand sidebar, and a right-hand sidebar for global results
  6. TO COME - Search Results Complete
    template for a basic search results page
  7. TO COME - No Search Results
    template for a page of zero results

Sample Level 3 implementations

These examples show implementations of sites making use of only part of the EBI visual guidelines or technical framework.

  1. TO COME - Bootstrap
    For services aiming for full visual compliance but not using the Foundation Framework base.
  2. TO COME - No JS
    Using the Foundation base framework with JavaScript or jQuery.
  3. TO COME - No JS or Foundation
    You won't benefit from any of the reusable components, but here's a bare bones visual implentation.
